Before we get started, thanks for choosing Documenting Force to help you improve your report writing skills. What follows below are some simple steps to get you ready to take the Documenting Force Writing Assessment.








How it works.

High level stuff...

The Writing Assessment will test your ability to watch a short video and document what transpires in report form. From the moment the video ends, you will have 60 minutes to file your report. We recommend that you eliminate all distractions for the duration of the Writing Assessment.








Testing Environment

The nuts and bolts...

Finally, the testing environment includes a notes panel along side the video screen. Because you can type notes during the video, we believe the best experience is on a laptop or desktop computer.

We have set up a practice area where you can get a feel for the testing environment before you take the live test. The testing environment consists of a 30 second video and a note panel. When the video is complete you will write a synopsis of the video in the report area. This will not be graded.

So, if you are on a laptop or desktop and have eliminated all distractions click 'Let's Practice'. If you are still not ready, click 'Come Back Later' and we'll email a link to you that will direct you back to the start of the instructions where you can pick back up.
