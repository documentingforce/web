Get it WRITE™

If it's not on paper, it did not happen.

How well do you write?

Our writing assessment evaluates individual powers of observation, recall, note taking, and narrative compilation skills. Personalized results provide specific feedback designed to help you:

1. identify missing and unclear content
2. accurately recall events to produce official reports
3. explain event details using clear and concise language

How does it work?

After registering, you will watch a professionally produced video scenario. The video is in color, includes audio, and is less than 3 minutes in length. After watching the video and taking notes, you have 60 minutes to write a narrative describing the video contents. That's all! Your personalized results will be sent to you. Knowledge gained through testing allows our team of adult learning experts to deliver targeted solutions and improve overall writing skills. Our goal is to help law enforcement professionals produce reports that are immune to unnecessary scrutiny and stand the test of time.
